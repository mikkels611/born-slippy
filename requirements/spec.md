##  Requirements for sequence pattern in sequence playback
Here are the requirements for the sequencer function when Play patterns in sequence is selected. 
Call the ememory slots patterns
Allow playback of minimum 1 bar per patter before move to next
If a pattern is selected during auto playback, then move to the selected pattern next and continue from there. Give the next pattern to play a glow or stippled line for user to see waht is next. 
When long press the play button, reset the auto pattern sequence playback to start from pattern 1 when press play again. 
When auto sequence is at the last pattern with music/notes, loop back to first pattern.
